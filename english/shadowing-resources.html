Shadowing is a useful technique for improving your pronunciation. On this page, you will find links to resources, mostly YouTube videos, that you can use for shadowing native speakers of British English.

Resources Related to Learning English

  • How to Start Conversations With "Small Talk" (ETJEnglish on YouTube, 5 minutes, 19.04.2016).
  • Learn English For FREE (ETJEnglish on YouTube, 7 minutes, 16.05.2016).
  • The Best Way To Improve English Speaking: Make Mistakes! (ETJEnglish on YouTube, 5 minutes, 19.05.2016).
  • How To Learn English FASTER: 3 Steps (ETJEnglish on YouTube, 7 minutes, 19.09.2016).
  • How To Remember What You Learn (ETJEnglish on YouTube, 6 minutes, 02.10.2016).
  • How To Keep A Conversation Going: English Lesson (ETJEnglish on YouTube, 6 minutes, 24.09.2016).
  • British Manners | English Lesson (ETJEnglish on YouTube, 7 minutes, 25.11.2016).
  • Small Talk Questions About Careers | British English (ETJEnglish on YouTube, 6 minutes, 11.05.2018).
  • How to Speak English Confidently | British Pronunciation Tips (ETJEnglish on YouTube, 7 minutes, 25.05.2018).

Miscellaneout Topics

  • How did medieval seafarers turn trees into boat parts? (FutureLearn on YouTube, 5 minutes, 28.05.2018). Professor Jon Adams of the Centre for Maritime Archaeology at the Unversity of Southampton talks about the techniques and technologies used by medieval shipwrights.
  • 45 Million Red Crabs March &dmdash; Lands of the Monsoon (BBC Earth on YouTube, 4 minutes, 23.04.2015).
    The voice-over in this video is relatively slow and there are many pauses in between the sentences. (The video has no subtitles.)
  • Defending the ant nest from intruders — Ant Attack (BBC Earth on YouTube, 4 minutes, 12.03.2010).

Resources Related to Game of Thrones

Game of Thrones is a fantasy television series. In edition to the episodes themselves, there are also a number of short animated sequences under the title Histories and Lore. These videos are spoken by actors from the television series, who are mostly native speakers of Britis English. The actors use a variety of accents; not all of them are easy to “shadow”. The actors with the clearest pronunciation include Charles Dance (Tywinn Lannister), Jonathan Pryce (the High Sparrow), Iain Glen (Jorah Mormont), Harry Lloyd (Viserys Targaryen), Conleth Hill (Varys), Paul Kaye (Thoros of Myr) and Mark Addy (Robert Baratheon).
